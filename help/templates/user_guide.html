  User Guide
  Overview of eConvenor

1.  eConvenor is a tool which helps the convenor/secretary of a group save time and be organised.

2.  eConvenor uses email to communicate with your group's participants. Participants don't get an eConvenor account because they don't need one. This keeps things simple for everyone.

3.  eConvenor produces clear, well-structured meeting agendas and minutes.

4.  eConvenor tracks tasks assigned to your group's participants and automatically sends them email reminders when their tasks are becoming due.

5.  eConvenor keeps an easy-to-navigate list of all the decisions your groups makes.

  About this User Guide

From here on, the user guide is organised by menu item.

If you can't find the information you're looking for in this document, try the Q&A which you'll find under the Help menu. If you still can't find your answer there, don't hesitate to drop us an email with your question.

  Dashboard

1.  The dashboard shows you your group's most important information at a glance.

2.  You can click the title bar of each task section and meeting section to go directly to that area.

3.  The two lowest sections keep you up-to-date with changes and improvements to eConvenor.

  Meetings

New meeting (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a page where you set up a new meeting.

2.  Fill in the details of the meeting and click Save meeting. You will then be taken to the Create agenda page.

Current meetings (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a page showing the meetings which need work done on them.

2.  Click the red button to work on the next task for a meeting on this page.

3.  Click the grey button to see the other things you can do with a meeting on this page.

Archived meetings (menu item)

1.  Once you've sent out the minutes for a meeting it is automatically archived. You can find it through this menu item.

2.  The agendas and minutes of archived meetings can be viewed by using the red buttons.

3.  If you need to change the agenda or minutes of an archived meeting, you must un-archive it first using the grey button. Un-archived meetings can then be found under the Current meetings menu item.

Create agenda (page)

1.  Click on an agenda item in the Navigation toolbar on the right of the screen to go to it.

2.  Change the order of agenda items by dragging them in the Navigation toolbar.

3.  Add an agenda item with the Add an item button in the Actions toolbar.

4.  Save your changes with the Save changes button in the Actions toolbar. Note that all data on this page is automatically saved every 60 seconds anyway.

5. When you are ready to send the agenda out, click the Preview and send button in the Actions toolbar. This will take you to the Review and send agenda page.

Review and send agenda (page)

1.  This page shows you exactly what your agenda will look like when it is sent out.

2.  Click on a page under the Navigation toolbar on the right of the screen to go to it.

3.  Click on Zoom in or Zoom out under the Actions toolbar to make the agenda preview larger or smaller.

4.  Click on Make changes under the Actions toolbar to return to the Create agenda page and edit the agenda.

5.  Click on Send later under the Actions toolbar to save the agenda in its current state and go to the dashboard.

6.  Click on Open in PDF format under the Actions toolbar to open a PDF file of the agenda in its current state. This is useful if you'd like to read the document with text-to-speech software.

7. To send out the agenda, click on To all participants or To selected participants under the Send agenda toolbar and follow the prompts. You will receive a success message when the agenda has been sent.

Create minutes (page)

1.  Click on an item under the Navigation toolbar on the right of the screen to go to it.

2.  Add a minutes item with the Add an item button in the Actions toolbar. Note that unlike adding an item for an agenda, new minutes items appear at the bottom of the minutes and can't be re-ordered.

3.  For each decision which is made, click the Add decision button for that item, and fill the details of the decision into the field that appears.

4.  For each task assigned to a participant, click the Add task button for that item, and fill the details of the task into the fields which appear.

5.  Save your changes with the Save changes button in the Actions toolbar. Note that all data on this page is automatically saved every 60 seconds anyway.

6. When you are ready to send the minutes, click the Preview and send button in the Actions toolbar. This will take you to the Review and send minutes page.

Review and send minutes (page)

1.  This page shows you exactly what your minutes will look like when they are sent out.

2.  Click on a page under the Navigation toolbar on the right of the screen to go to it.

3.  Click on Zoom in or Zoom out under the Actions toolbar to make the minutes preview larger or smaller.

4.  Click on Make changes under the Actions toolbar to return to the Create minutes page and edit the minutes.

5.  Click on Send later under the Actions toolbar to save the minutes in their current state and go to the dashboard.

6.  Click on Open in PDF format under the Actions toolbar to open a PDF file of the minutes in their current state. This is useful if you'd like to read the document with text-to-speech software.

7.  To send out the minutes, click on To all participants or To selected participants under the Send minutes toolbar and follow the prompts. You will receive a success message when the minutes have been sent.

  Tasks

New task (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a page to add a new task for a participant. This is how you assign a task to a participant outside of a meeting.

Manage tasks (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a page which shows your group's tasks. The Filter options on the right side of the screen allow you to view overdue, incomplete or completed tasks.

2.  Clicking on a task allows you to edit it.

3.  When a task has been completed, change its status to 'Completed'.

4.  Tasks assigned in a meeting will only appear in these lists once that meeting's minutes have been distributed.

  Decisions

View decisions (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a page which shows your group's decisions. A decision appears in this list once the minutes of the meeting in which it was made have been distributed.

  Participants

New participant (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a page where you can add a new participant.

Manage participants (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a page which shows your group's participants.

2.  The Filter options on the right side of the screen allow you to view active, inactive or former participants.

3.  Clicking on a participant shows you their details and a list of tasks assigned to them which are incomplete. The participant's details can be edited by clicking on the red button on this page.

4.  Although we advise against it, automatic task reminder emails for a participant can be switched off by editing their details and de-selecting the checkbox labelled "Automatically email reminders about nearly-due and overdue tasks (recommended)".

  Feedback

Report a bug (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a form where you can report a bug within eConvenor. Please use this form to tell us if eConvenor ever does something unexpected. If we don't know about problems, we can't fix them!

Request a feature/change (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a form where you can request a feature or function which you would like to see in eConvenor.

View all bug reports (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a list of bug reports made by all eConvenor users.

View all feature/change requests (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a list of feature/change requests submitted by all eConvenor users.

  Help

Under this menu item there are links to the Quick-Start Guide, this User Guide, the Questions and Answers (Q&A), and instructions for asking a question about how eConvenor works.

  The 'Hi' menu

Account settings (menu item)

1.  This takes you to a page which shows your account settings. These can be edited using the red buttons.

2.  Your password can be changed by clicking the Change your password button on this page.

Logout (menu item)

1.  Logs you out of eConvenor.

  Changing the text size

The text in eConvenor can be made larger by holding down the control/command key and pressing the "+" key at the same time.

The text in eConvenor can be made smaller by holding down the control/command key and pressing the "-" key at the same time.
